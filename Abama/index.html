All you need to know about FREELANCING!

If you are just coming across the word freelancing for the first time, you'd probably be wondering what it means. Come with me as I take you through the world of freelancing.
As you must have figured out by now, a person involved in freelancing is reffered to as a FREELANCER.
Freelancing refers to the act of working for organsations or individuals from remote places mostly for short duration. Often times in Freelancing communication is achieved with the internet via the different social media platforms. Freelancers are hired for various types of services. These services are categorized into three (3) main niches. These are:

  1. writing,
  2. Graphics
  3. E-mail marketing.
